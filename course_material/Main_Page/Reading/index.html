Reference Texts

Our main driver for reading will be the following (online) book:
Speech and Language Processing (3rd ed. draft august 24) Dan Jurafsky and James H. Martin
The second edition is available as a physical purchase. It's a really good source if you're interested in NLP, but difficult to come by, does not feature an extensive DNN chapter, and we will only cover a small portion.
